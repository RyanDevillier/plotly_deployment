Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Consumer-friendly food has been synthesized from many unusual sources, such as algae and fungi. Improbable Beef, a company whose goal is to revolutionize the synthesis of edible meat products by finding bacterial species that produce proteins that taste like beef, has asked for volunteers in their new research project. The goal of the project is to swab the bacteria from volunteers' navels and test to determine if these bacterial samples could be ultimately used to synthesize edible beef products. This dashboard contains the data for each participant in this bacterial study. To see specific participants' results, click the dropdown menu in the table next to "Test Subject ID No.:" and choose the ID number of the desired participant. The dashboard will automatically generate multiple graphs displaying the bacteria information found in the chosen participant's belly button, as well the demographic information for that participant.
